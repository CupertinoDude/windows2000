Message Queuing

Message Queuing enables applications running at different times to communicate across platforms and networks that may be temporarily offline.

You can use Computer Management or Active Directory Users and Computers to administer Message Queuing.

Computer Management

To use Active Directory Users and Computers to administer Message Queuing
1. Open Active Directory Users and Computers.
2. Click View and click View Users, Groups and Computers as containers.
3. Click View and click Advanced Features.
