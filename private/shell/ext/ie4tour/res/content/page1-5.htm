Communicate and Collaborate with Others Using NetMeeting
With NetMeeting, you can use data, audio, and video to communicate and collaborate with others in real-time over the Internet. Work together with multiple people by sharing applications for real-time collaboration, draw diagrams and exchange pictures using an electronic whiteboard, send messages with a text-based chat program, and exchange files during a conference. You can also communicate using voice over the Internet, and even let others see you using a video camera attached to your PC.

         
To use NetMeeting, click the Start menu, point to Programs, point to Internet Explorer, and then click NetMeeting (if you don't see it listed, you can install it from the Internet Explorer 4.0 website).
