Improvements to Search and History make finding Web sites the first time (and ever after!) easier. To open or close the Search or History bar, click its button on the toolbar.

The Search bar lets you choose your favorite search engine and then displays results right there. These links stay in view as you view the Web sites in the right side of the browser window. The new History bar organizes the Web pages you have previously visited by date and makes finding a page you want to return to much easier. The links stay in the bar while you view the contents in the right side of the window.
